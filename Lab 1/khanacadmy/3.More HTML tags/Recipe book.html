Recipe #1

Teriyaki chicken and rice

  • Time: 25 - 40min
  • Serves: 2 - 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken fillet - 600 g
Long-grain rice - 250 g
Ginger root - 10 g
Garlic - 4 cloves
Classic soy sauce - 50 ml
Vegetable oil - 2 tbsp.
Rice vinegar - 1 tbsp.
Cornstarch - 1 tbsp.
Ground black pepper - 1/2 tsp.
Red chili pepper flakes - 1/3 tsp.
Water - 600 ml
Ground cinnamon - 1/3 tsp.
Salt - 1/2 tsp.
Sugar - 3 tbsp.

Step 1: Cut the chicken fillet into 4-5 cm pieces, chop the ginger and garlic.


Step 2:Put 250 g of thoroughly washed rice in a saucepan, add 500 ml of water, stir, bring to a boil, reduce the heat to low, cover and cook for 15 minutes, then turn off the stove and leave the rice under a closed lid for another 5 minutes. Then, add rice vinegar with 1 tsp sugar and 1/2 tsp salt diluted in it, mix thoroughly with the rice. The garnish is ready.


Step 3:Heat a skillet over high heat with 2 tbsp of vegetable oil, add the sliced chicken pieces, and fry, stirring, over high heat for 5-7 minutes until golden.


Step 4:Reduce heat to medium, add ginger, garlic to the skillet, stir well and fry for 1 more minute.


Step 5:Then add ground black pepper, chili pepper flakes, ground cinnamon, 2.5 tbsp sugar, soy sauce to the pan. Dilute cornstarch in 100 ml of water, stir, pour into the pan, stirring, bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and cook for another 2 minutes.


Step 6:Sprinkle teriyaki chicken with sesame seeds, green onions and hot peppers before serving. Serve hot with cooked rice.
